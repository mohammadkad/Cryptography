### PKCS 12 : https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/PKCS_12
In cryptography, PKCS #12 defines an archive file format for storing many cryptography objects as a single file (.p12, .pfx). It is commonly used to bundle a private key with its X.509 certificate or to bundle all the members of a chain of trust.
